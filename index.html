About Us

Divine Bookstore which officially opened in 2015 is one of the best bookstore in the whole of Lagos state and is ranked number 3 among the 50 best bookstore in Lagos.

Divine Bookstore offers;

  • Free coffee for customers
  • Friendly customer services
  • A well arranged book store for easy reference of various books

Our hours are 10am to 9pm weekdays, 12pm to 7pm on weekends.
